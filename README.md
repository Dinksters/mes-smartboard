# 🏫 MES Smartboard (Class 7A Portal)

An interactive, all-in-one web-based classroom portal and digital dashboard custom-tailored for the students and teachers of class 7A at **MES Campus School, Kuttippuram**. 

Live Demo: [View MES Smartboard](https://dinksters.github.io/mes-smartboard)

---

## 🌟 Key Features

* **🧠 Custom MES AI Assistant:** Integrated chatbot capable of being pre-trained with school syllabus materials to assist teachers with rapid lesson planning.
* **🪄 Blank Endless Canvas:** An interactive digital whiteboard equipped with adjustable pen sizes (Fine, Medium, Thick), shape-drawing utilities, and eraser controls.
* **📚 Localized Academic Hub:** Quick-access navigation tabs split neatly by subject syllabi:
  * **Languages:** English, Hindi, and Malayalam.
  * **Science & Math:** Mathematics (Part 1 & 2) and General Science.
  * **Social Science:** Core social studies syllabus links.
* **🌐 Cross-Device Optimization:** Built entirely on modern web technologies ensuring flawless performance on classroom smartboards, desktop computers, and student tablets.
